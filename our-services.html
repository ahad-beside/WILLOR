The Willor Trust Advantage

In our practice, we emphasize objectivity and take a very cost-efficient approach to intelligent investment selection and management. Our investment style is active management.

 

We do not subject our client to the hidden fees often found in mutual funds, limited partnerships, or other “packaged” products. Instead, we will analyze your financial needs and help you regain control of your finances through a personalized portfolio of equities and exchange-traded funds (ETF’s).

 

It is our responsibility to you, as our client, to develop a personal investment plan, to monitor and manage the performance results of your portfolio, and report your account performance regularly. We conduct an annual review of your accounts, consistently considering your changing needs. We are a fee-based asset manager.

 

All advice, services, and ongoing asset management are included in a nominal flat rate, thereby eliminating all commission incentives, in-house sale gimmicks, or referral fees. Rest assured, your financial future, peace of mind, and best interests are always our primary focus!

Account Size

 

In order to best serve our clients’ needs through personalized attention and services, our minimum investment per account is EUR 2,500,000. This minimum may be reduced in select situations.
